Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

Kage collects the following data to function properly:

  • User IDs: To identify users for commands, economy, and permissions.
  • Server IDs: To store server-specific configurations (prefixes, welcome messages).
  • Command Usage: We log command usage anonymously to improve performance.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used strictly for:

  • Executing requested commands.
  • Maintaining the economy system (credits, inventory).
  • preventing abuse and ensuring security.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data. To do so, please join our Support Server and open a ticket.

5. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of changes.
